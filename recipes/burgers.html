Double ranch western burger

  • Pretzel bun
  • Chipotle ranch
  • Red onion
  • Grass fed burger patty
  • Pepperjack Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Bacon
  • Tomato
  • Optional side of fries
  1. Toast your pretzel bun
  2. Spread chipotle ranch on one half of toasted bun
  3. Place tomatoes on bottom half, followed by grass fed patty
  4. Optional: melt your pepperjack cheese on the grass fed patty
  5. Add lettuce, bacon and red onion on top of patty
  6. Place top of bun stack of deliciousness and enjoy
