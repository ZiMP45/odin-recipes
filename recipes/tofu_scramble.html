Tofu Scramble

Hearty tofu scramble with veggies

  • 1 package extra firm tofu
  • 1 cup crimini mushrooms
  • Tumeric
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Water
  • Avocado oil
  1. Pre-heat skillet on medium-high heat
  2. Chop mushrooms finely
  3. Drain excess water from tofu
  4. Add oil to pan and sautee mushrooms
  5. Crumble tofu into pan with mushrooms and sautee until they have some color
  6. Add in your seasonings and 1 tbsp of water and mix until evenly coated
  7. Heat until warm and then sprinkle nutritional yeast on top (gives a cheesy texture)
  8. Optional: add in veggies of choice for additional color and healthiness
