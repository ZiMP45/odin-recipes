Protein Pancakes

Simple ingredient protein pancakes

  • 3 eggs
  • Banana
  • 1 cup oats
  • Cinnamon
  • Optional: vanilla extract
  • Optional: 1 scoop protein powder
  1. Pre-warm your skillet or griddle
  2. Add eggs, banana, oats, cinnamon, vanilla and protein to blender
  3. Blend until smooth consistency
  4. Pour 5-inch circle into skillet or griddle
  5. Flip when edges are dry and bubbles have formed on pancake
  6. Enjoy with fruit or syrup
